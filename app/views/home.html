Why Join This Club?

Relationships

The ability to network with a cross section of leaders in business, medicine, law, and academia, who help each other, and collectively help others, while promoting lasting friendships.

Prestige

Rotary, particularly in South Korea, is composed of leaders of many businesses and professions that influence policy and help guide our community.

Service

Rotary is a service club that lets you make a difference as part of the world's largest family of givers. The service culture of an online club is no different, and we strive to provide the best and most impactful service opportunities for our club members.

How Does This Club Meet?

Via Our State-of-the-Art Meeting Platform

Meeting Platform

Step 1

Current club members click the LOGIN button in the navigation bar. From there, they sign into the Global Bridges Rotary Dashboard.

Step 2

Inside of the Dashboard, club members can do a variety of things, from attending a weekly club meeting, to participating on a committee, to starting a discussion, all while networking and building relationships

Step 3

Click the LOGOUT button when you are ready to leave, and that's it!
